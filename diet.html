Comprehensive Nutrition Guidelines

Category Guidelines
Hydration - Minimum 3 liters of water daily
- Increase intake during workouts
- Monitor urine color for hydration status
Supplementation - Consider adding creatine monohydrate
- Daily multivitamin recommended
- Omega-3 supplements if needed
Meal Timing - Follow meal schedule consistently
- Adjust timing based on workout schedule
- Allow 2-3 hours between major meals
Cheat Meals - One cheat meal per week allowed
- Stay within daily calorie limit
- Plan cheat meals around workouts
Progress Monitoring - Track weight weekly
- Take measurements every 2-3 weeks
- Adjust calories if not gaining muscle
Food Quality - Choose whole, unprocessed foods
- Prioritize lean protein sources
- Include variety of fruits and vegetables
