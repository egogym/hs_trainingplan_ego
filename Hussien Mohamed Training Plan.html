Training Tips & Guidelines

Category Guidelines
Warm-up Protocol - 5-10 minutes of light cardio to increase body temperature
- Dynamic stretches for major muscle groups
- 2-3 warm-up sets before first exercise
Form & Technique - Watch tutorial videos before attempting new exercises
- Focus on mind-muscle connection
- Control the negative (eccentric) portion of each rep
Progressive Overload - Increase weight by 2.5-5 lbs when you can complete all reps
- Maintain proper form before increasing weight
- Track your workouts to ensure progression
Rest & Recovery - Get 7-9 hours of sleep per night
- Stay hydrated (1 gallon water daily)
- Take rest days when needed for recovery
Nutrition - Consume 1.6-2.2g protein per kg bodyweight
- Eat in slight caloric surplus (300-500 calories)
- Pre-workout meal 2-3 hours before training
Cardio Guidelines - Maintain steady pace for full 25 minutes
- Keep heart rate in target zone (130-150 BPM)
- Stay consistent with daily cardio
